# Singapore Government Knowledge Ingestion List

## Feed these URLs into your MPS-AI-Agent knowledge graph

Send each URL to your agent with the command: `ingest this: [URL]`

Work through agencies in priority order. Start with the ones most common at MPS. The knowledge graph builds incrementally — you do not need to do this all at once.

---

## HOW TO INGEST

In your WhatsApp or Telegram chat with the agent:

```
ingest this: https://www.hdb.gov.sg/buying-a-flat/flat-grant-and-loan-eligibility
```

Or batch-ingest by sending a list:

```
ingest these URLs:
1. https://www.hdb.gov.sg/...
2. https://www.cpf.gov.sg/...
3. https://www.msf.gov.sg/...
```

After ingesting, test with:

```
what do I know about HDB Enhanced CPF Housing Grant?
what are the ComCare eligibility criteria?
```

Run these test queries to confirm the knowledge graph is working:

```
What are the income ceilings for HDB grants for a first-timer family?

A constituent cannot afford their hospital bill. Walk me through the options.

What is the difference between ComCare SMTA and Long-Term Assistance?

A PR application was rejected. What should the MP letter focus on?

What changed for CPF in 2026?

A foreign spouse on LTVP wants to work. What are the options?
```

If the agent answers accurately and cites the right agencies and schemes, your knowledge base is well-loaded.
